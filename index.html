Scribewise

Record, transcribe, and summarize. Privately, on your Mac.

Scribewise captures your microphone and your computer's audio, turns it into an accurate transcript, and writes you a clean summary. Everything runs on-device, so your conversations never leave your Mac.

Download for macOS

Free. Requires macOS 15 (Sequoia) or later on Apple Silicon.

Fully on-device

Transcription and summaries run locally. No accounts, no uploads, no cloud.

Mic and system audio

Capture both sides of a call or meeting, then get speaker-separated notes.

Updates itself

Scribewise quietly checks for new versions and updates with one click.
